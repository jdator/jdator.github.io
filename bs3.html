 The object I created is an icon that represents one of my favorite rappers/artists, Capital STEEZ. The crown and the eye symbolize parts of the chakra system and the different centers of energy one can focus on to achieve spiritual balance. STEEZ used this crown symbol throughout his album art and just like his music, it has immortalized his legacy even after his suicide in 2012.
