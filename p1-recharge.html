Recharge

Video

 My video Recharge demonstrates a regular day in the life of a college student in 2020 and how spending too much screen time without regular breaks can be draining to our own energy. We spend an average of at least 1-4 hours a day on zoom calls on top of our TV/entertainment, gaming, and social media intake, and just like our devices, we also have a maximum capacity we can operate at before we run out of battery. Taking care of our physical/mental health and connecting back with nature can help us recharge and increase our happiness and wellbeing.
