Meme mashup

The first image is a quick meme mashup we created in class. A catchier name my coworker suggested for it was, "Caucasian Confusion". For the second mashup, I used the dog from the classic "This is fine" meme and paired it with an image that addresses the lack of news coverage on the Amazon rainforest fire. I layered on a picture of Trump and Jair Bolsonaro, Brazil's president, to show their ignorance to this situation. Never underestimate the power of memes.
